Milk Steak Recipe

Description

Milksteak is a meal enjoyed by Charlie Kelly. It was first mentioned in the Season 5 episode "The Waitress is Getting Married" where Charlie wants to put it down as his favorite food in an online dating profile. It is later mentioned in the same episode as Charlie orders it in the restaurant.

It was mentioned again in the Season 13 episode "Charlie's Home Alone" as one of the brown foods Charlie eats as part of his color rituals to make the Eagles win.

Ingredients

  • 8oz steak
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2oz of raw jelly beans

Steps

  1. Pour milk into pot and bring to a boil
  2. Put steak into boiling pot of milk
  3. Occassionally stir to allow even cooking of steak
  4. Once boiled over hard, remove steak from pot and serve on dish
  5. Add raw jelly beans as garnish
  6. Enjoy!
